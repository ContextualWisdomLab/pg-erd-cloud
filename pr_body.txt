## 💡 What
Added keyboard submission (`Enter` key) and a delete confirmation dialog to the `EditEdgeModal` component.
Added a unit test for `EditEdgeModal` to maintain 100% test coverage and ensure `pnpm run coverage` passes accurately.

## 🎯 Why
- **Keyboard navigation:** Modal inputs without standard wrapping `<form>` elements inherently lack keyboard submission support, forcing users to switch from keyboard to mouse just to complete simple forms.
- **Accidental deletion:** In the ERD canvas, destructive actions like deleting relations were missing user confirmation, increasing the chance of accidental data loss.

## 📸 Before/After
- **Before:** Pressing 'Enter' in the input did nothing. Clicking "삭제" immediately deleted the relationship without warning.
- **After:** Pressing 'Enter' saves the changes. Clicking "삭제" triggers a native `window.confirm` dialog to prevent accidental deletion.

## ♿ Accessibility
- Added `aria-label="관계 삭제"` to the delete button, providing better context for screen reader users compared to just the visible text ("삭제").
